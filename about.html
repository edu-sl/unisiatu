About Our Institution

Unisiatu Information and Technology Services

UNISIATU Information and Technology Institute is a modern learning center in Lungi Sierra Leone dedicated to equipping students with essential digital skills. The institution focuses on practical training, innovation, and career development in the field of Information Technology.

Our mission: is to empower young minds with real-world technological knowledge through hands-on experience, preparing them for global opportunities in the digital economy.
